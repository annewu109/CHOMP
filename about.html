About Us

Welcome to CHOMP — your partner in making healthy eating simple, efficient, and stress-free.

At CHOMP (Consumer Health Oriented Meal Prepping), we believe that eating well shouldn't be a chore. That's why we’ve created a platform that streamlines meal prepping and grocery shopping into a single, easy process. With CHOMP, you can browse a variety of balanced, delicious meal options, add them to your plan, and instantly populate your grocery cart with exactly what you need. Then, with just a few clicks, your ingredients are delivered straight from the nearest grocery store to your doorstep — fresh, fast, and hassle-free.

Our mission is simple:

Take the stress out of cooking for yourself while supporting a healthier, more mindful lifestyle.

We designed CHOMP to tackle some of the biggest everyday challenges:

  • Meal fatigue — by offering a rotating selection of meals so you're never stuck eating the same thing over and over.
  • Food waste — by giving you exactly the right ingredients for the meals you choose, no random leftovers.
  • Expensive, unhealthy eating habits — by making it easier to cook at home instead of relying on costly takeout.
  • Portion control — with recipes designed to meet your specific needs, whether you're prepping for one or a family.
  • Decision fatigue — by handling the "what's for dinner?" question so you can spend your time on what matters most.

At CHOMP, we’re not just about meal prepping — we’re about empowering you to eat better, feel better, and live better.
Less waste. Less stress. More delicious, healthy meals — made easy.

Eat Smarter. Live Better. CHOMP Makes It Easy.
